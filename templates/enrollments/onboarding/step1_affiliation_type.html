Associated Affiliation

For persons who are informally or non-professionally involved in ministry, who want to be associated with the professional body.

Who This Is For:

  • • Volunteer ministry workers
  • • Church committee members
  • • Lay ministers and leaders
  • • Those exploring ministry

Designated Affiliation

For applicants with required SAQA registered qualifications and necessary experience to apply for professional designation.

Requirements:

  • • SAQA registered qualifications
  • • Professional ministry experience
  • • Supervision requirements
  • • Comprehensive application

Student Affiliation

For full-time or part-time ministry students who are not yet in formal ministry positions.

Who This Is For:

  • • Seminary students
  • • Bible college students
  • • Ministry training participants
  • • Pre-ministry candidates

Note: Those already in formal ministry should apply as designated affiliates.
